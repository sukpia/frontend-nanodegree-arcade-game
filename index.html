CLASSIC ARCADE GAME

FROM GRASS TO WATER

Successfully walk player into water without colliding any obstacles.

  1. Use keyboard's arrow keys to walk the player.
  2. If the player collides with any bugs, game will reset.
  3. Each forward step scores 10 points.
  4. Each collected gem scores 10 points.
  5. Each backward step deduct 10 points.

END OF GAME

  1. The game ends when the player reach the water.
  2. Your score and time will displayed.
  3. To Play Again: press Enter key.
